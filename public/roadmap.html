Alpha Release Announce

The pre-beta release (codename mArci) can boot, and has a limited shell. Although there's a long way to go, but now at least there's something to show. Under the hood lots of things are happening. In a micro-kernel architectue, considerable amount of coding had to be done just to get the character you type being displayed, including lfb based VT-compatible tty, stdin/stdout pipes etc.
